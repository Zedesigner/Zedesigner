FAQs

How much do your design services cost?
I customize my pricing based on your unique needs and project scope. Instead of offering standard packages, I create tailored solutions that align with your goals and budget. Reach out for a free consultation, and I'll provide a detailed quote for your project.
How long does a typical design project take to complete?
My timelines depend on the scope and complexity of your project. Generally, logo design takes 2-3 weeks, brand identity projects take 4-6 weeks, and web design projects take 5-6 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll give you a detailed timeline based on your specific project needs.
What is your design process like?
I follow a proven 4-step process: First, we begin with Discovery to understand your business, goals, and target audience. Then, I move to Conceptualization, exploring multiple creative directions. Next comes Refinement, where I perfect your chosen concept through feedback and iterations. Finally, we handle the Launch and provide all necessary files and ongoing support.
What files and deliverables will I receive?
You'll get all the necessary file formats for both digital and print use. For logos, this includes vector files (AI, EPS, SVG), raster files (PNG, JPEG), and various color variations. For brand identity projects, you'll receive a complete brand guide, and for web design projects, I'll provide all source files along with detailed documentation.
Do you offer revisions, and how many are included?
Yes, revisions are an important part of my process. I include multiple revision rounds in each project phase to ensure you're fully satisfied. My standard packages offer up to three revision rounds per phase, and I’ll clearly outline this in your project proposal.
